About Us

At Lamplight Digital, we are passionate about empowering local businesses in Leeds and beyond. Our mission is to provide modern, reliable, and tailored IT solutions and web development services to help small businesses thrive in a digital world.

We understand the unique challenges faced by small businesses and are dedicated to offering personalized support, whether it's designing a sleek website, implementing efficient IT systems, or providing cloud and hosting solutions. Our goal is to be your trusted partner in navigating technology opportunities and challenges.

Founded by James Vardy, a Leeds-based Web Developer and IT Specialist, Lamplight Digital combines technical expertise with a deep appreciation for the local community. James brings years of experience in web development, IT architecture, and cloud solutions, along with a passion for collaborating with businesses in various industries, including music, food, and more.

We believe in building strong relationships with our clients and delivering solutions that not only meet their needs but also exceed their expectations. Let's work together to illuminate your business's potential.
